# xAID — AI CT Reporting Service Pricing
Last updated: April 2026

## Pricing Model

- **Structure:** Per-study pricing — pay per CT report, no monthly minimums, no seat licenses
- **What's included:** Radiologist review on every report (not billed separately)
- **Pricing level:** Narrow AI rates — comparable to AI overlay solutions, despite including full radiologist review and ready-to-sign output

## Free Pilot

- **Cost:** Free
- **Studies:** 5 CT studies
- **Integration required:** No — send studies directly, no DICOM/HL7 setup needed
- **Commitment:** None
- **Coverage:** Any combination of head, chest, or abdomen CT
- **Output:** Full ready-to-sign reports

## Volume Pricing

- Pricing scales with monthly CT study volume
- No minimum study requirement to start
- Custom pricing for teleradiology providers and radiology group partnerships

## What's Included at All Tiers

- Ready-to-sign CT report — head, chest, or abdomen
- Two AI analysis layers (foundation model + secondary AI), 150+ findings per report
- In-house radiologist review and sign-off on every report
- 2–12 hour turnaround time (configurable SLA)
- Standard DICOM input / HL7 output
- 90% no-edit guarantee with financial reimbursement if below threshold
- HIPAA compliance, US-based AWS infrastructure

- Setup support — typically live in under one week
