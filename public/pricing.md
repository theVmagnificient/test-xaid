# xAID — AI CT Reporting Service Pricing
Last updated: July 2026

## Pricing Model

- **Structure:** Per-study pricing — pay per CT report, no monthly minimums, no seat licenses
- **What's included:** Radiologist review on every report (not billed separately)
- **Pricing level:** Narrow AI rates — comparable to AI overlay solutions, despite including full radiologist review and ready-to-sign output

## Free Pilot

- **Cost:** Free
- **Studies:** 5 CT studies
- **Integration required:** No — send studies directly, no DICOM/HL7 setup needed
- **Commitment:** None
- **Coverage:** Any combination of head, chest, or abdomen CT
- **Output:** Full ready-to-sign reports
